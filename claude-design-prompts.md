## PROMPT 1 — Match Reaction Post

```
Create a TikTok image post for Block 90', a street football channel.

Content:
- Competition: [e.g. Premier League · Matchday 34]
- Home team: [TEAM NAME], Score: [X]
- Away team: [TEAM NAME], Score: [X]
- Reaction headline: [Your punchy 1-2 sentence take]
- Status: [Full Time / Half Time / Live]

Brand rules — follow exactly:
- Background: #0A0A0A (deep black), no exceptions
- Top accent bar: 3px solid #D62828 (Bloc Red)
- Score font: Bebas Neue, large, #F0EDE8
- Headline font: Barlow Condensed 700, uppercase, #F0EDE8
- One key word in the headline → colour it #D62828
- Competition/eyebrow text: Barlow Condensed 600, 9px, #F4A01C, letter-spacing 3px
- Ghost background element: the number "90" in huge Bebas Neue, #D62828 at 6% opacity, bottom-right
- Bottom footer: wordmark "BLOCK 90'" left (90' in red), status badge right
- Format: 9:16 vertical (1080×1920px proportions)
- No gradients, no rounded corners on the card, no drop shadows
```

## QUICK RULES — Brand Dos & Don'ts

**Always:**
- Deep Black background (#0A0A0A) on all posts
- Barlow Condensed for all headlines (uppercase)
- Bebas Neue for scores, large numbers, wordmark only
- Thin 0.5px dividers (#2E2E2E) — never thick lines except accent bars
- Wordmark "BLOCK 90'" in footer — "90'" always in #D62828

**Never:**
- White or light backgrounds
- More than 3 colours in one post
- Rounded card corners
- Drop shadows or glows
- Body text in gold (gold = eyebrows and labels only)
- Gradients of any kind
